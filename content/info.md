#### About me. 
Hi. I’m Tyler. I’m usually a professional [copywriter](https://www.linkedin.com/in/tylerhellard/), but sometimes I write [other things](/writing/), too. Once I wrote a [novel](/terrypunchout/) that (incredibly!) became a Canadian best seller (impossibly!) eight years after it came out.

#### About Pop Loser. 
Pop Loser is a blog (and, for awhile, newsletter) I've written on and off since 2007. It's changed a lot over the years and I've burned it all to the ground five or six times. The most recent [iteration](/poploser/) is a bit esoteric, with a little writing and a lot of links, and includes "[mixtapes](/mixtapes/)." When each volume finishes, posts are collected on an omnibus page: Vol. [1](/vol1/), [2](/vol2/), [3](/vol3/), [4](/vol4/), [5](/vol5/). Most previous versions of Pop Loser are archived in the following places: Newsletters #1–78 (unavailable), [#79–97](https://web.archive.org/web/20220312222936/https://us2.campaign-archive.com/home/?u=73477be0867f67a19f1fec465&id=f5d72195cb) and [#98–113](https://web.archive.org/web/20200809173730/https://www.getrevue.co/profile/poploser/#archive) (2014–2018). [Original blog](https://web.archive.org/web/20240000000000*/http://poploser.org/) ([2007](https://web.archive.org/web/20070528205939/http://www.spincity.org/blog/)–[2014](https://web.archive.org/web/20140106063005/http://poploser.org/)) and [Tumblr](https://poploser.tumblr.com/) clone ([2010–2014](https://poploser.tumblr.com/archive)). From 2019 to 2024, I probably had better things to do.

#### Tech stuff.
As of July 2026, this website is powered by [Hugo](https://gohugo.io/) using a modified version of [Monochrome](https://kaiiiz.github.io/hugo-theme-monochrome/) and hosted on [GitHub](https://github.com/poploser/tylerhellard.com). The type is set primarily in [Monda](https://github.com/vernnobile/mondaFont) with icons, including the many skulls, come from [Font Awesome](https://fontawesome.com/). Basic stats are collected with [GoatCounter](https://www.goatcounter.com/) (most options turned off — I'm only getting basic page views and some limited referral data). All content is written and edited in markdown with [IA Writer](https://ia.net/writer). Here are your <span style="color: #fff; background: #FE5C00; padding: 0 5px;">**RSS**</span> options: 🚒 [Firehose](https://tylerhellard.com/feed.xml) (blog posts, other writing, mixtapes, photos, etc.) / 📰 [Pop Loser](https://tylerhellard.com/poploser.xml) (just the blog posts) / 🎧 [Pod Loser](	https://media.rss.com/podloser/feed.xml) (a podcast feed for just the mixtapes).

#### Elsewhere. 
I went scorched earth on social media a few years ago, but have crept back onto [Mastodon](https://mstdn.ca/@poploser) (though I accidentally nuked all my follows and followers in a bout of Fediverse transfer stupidity in June 2026) and [Bluesky](https://bsky.app/). Most posts to this site also filter out to those sites and sometimes I even scroll those feeds and "engage" with people. Sometimes.
