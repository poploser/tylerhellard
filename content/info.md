#### About me. 
Hi. I’m Tyler. I’m usually a professional [copywriter](https://www.linkedin.com/in/tylerhellard/), but sometimes I write [other things](TKTK), too. Once I wrote a [novel](TKTK) that (incredibly!) became a Canadian [best seller](TKTK) (impossibly!) eight years after it came out. Someday I'll write another one.

#### About Pop Loser. 
Pop Loser is a blog (and, for awhile, newsletter) I've written on and off since 2007. It's changed a lot over the years and I've burned it all to the ground five or six times. The most recent [iteration](TKTK) is a bit esoteric, with a little writing and a lot of links, and includes "[mixtapes](TKTK)." When each volume finishes, posts are collected on an omnibus page: Vol. [1](TKTK), [2](TKTK), [3](TKTK), [4](TKTK), [5](TKTK). Most previous versions of Pop Loser are archived in the following places: Newsletters #1–78 (unavailable), [#79–97](https://web.archive.org/web/20220312222936/https://us2.campaign-archive.com/home/?u=73477be0867f67a19f1fec465&id=f5d72195cb), and [#98–113](https://web.archive.org/web/20200809173730/https://www.getrevue.co/profile/poploser/#archive) (2014–2018). [Original blog](https://web.archive.org/web/20240000000000*/http://poploser.org/) ([2007](https://web.archive.org/web/20070528205939/http://www.spincity.org/blog/)–[2014](https://web.archive.org/web/20140106063005/http://poploser.org/)) and [Tumblr](https://poploser.tumblr.com/) clone ([2010–2014](https://poploser.tumblr.com/archive)). From 2019 to 2024, I probably had better things to do.

#### Tech stuff.
As of 2026, this website is powered by [Hugo](https://gohugo.io/) using a modified version of [Monochrome](https://kaiiiz.github.io/hugo-theme-monochrome/) and hosted on [GitHub](https://github.com/poploser/tylerhellard.com). Basic stats are collected with [GoatCounter](https://www.goatcounter.com/) (with most options turned off). All content is written and edited in markdown with [IA Writer](TKTK). Here are your <span style="color: #fff; background: #FE5C00; padding: 0 5px;">**RSS**</span> options: 🚒 [Firehose](TKTK) (blog posts, field notes (f.k.a. "tweets"), mixtapes, photos, etc.) / 📰 [Pop Loser](TKTK) (just the blog posts) / 🎧 [Pod Loser](TKTK) (a podcast feed for just the mixtapes) / 📸 [Photos](TKTK) (hi mom!) / 👾 [High Scores](TKTK) (updated personal bests on a handful of classic arcade games).
